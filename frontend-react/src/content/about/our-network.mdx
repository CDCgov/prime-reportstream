### SimpleReport

Our network expands beyond the organizations directly using ReportStream and includes the testing sites
and providers using SimpleReport as well. Data reported by using [SimpleReport](https://www.simplereport.gov),
another CDC product, is sent to public health agencies through ReportStream.

Learn more about how SimpleReport and ReportStream [work together](/managing-your-connection/refer-healthcare-organizations)
to provide healthcare organizations with reporting tools that meet their needs.
