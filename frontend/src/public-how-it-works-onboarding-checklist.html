If you’re a public health department and want to connect ReportStream through Electronic Lab Reporting (ELR), you’ll need to fill out the ReportStream ELR onboarding form.

Our checklist here provides a preview of what we’ll ask, so you can gather everything you need to complete the form.

ELR onboarding form Contact us

On this page:

- ELR contact information - Program or admin staff contact information - Data requirements and preferences - Testing facility registration - HL7 data fields (Not applicable if using an alternate data format - SFTP details - Document uploads

Before gathering information on the checklist or completing the ELR onboarding form, we recommend first reviewing information on our Getting started page and the technical details outlined in Systems & settings with your IT and data specialists.

### ELR contact information This is the person who helps set up the ELR connection. It is often an IT person or a third party agency. - Name - Email address - Phone number ### Program or admin staff contact information - Name - Email address - Phone number ### Data requirements and preferences ReportStream sends data as an [HL7](http://why.hl7.org/) file via Secure File Transfer Protocol (SFTP). We capture Health and Human Services (HHS) required fields, including “Ask on Order Entry” questions. Please note: ReportStream follows industry standards for [data formatting](https://github.com/CDCgov/prime-data-hub/blob/production/prime-router/docs/schema_documentation/primedatainput-pdi-covid-19.md) and reporting. While we can support custom format or sending mechanisms, this will increase the time required to build your connection. Confirm your preferences and provide details for: - **File type:** (HL7 v2.5.1, CSV, or other) - **Transport method:** (SFTP, VPN, Health Information Exchange, SOAP, PHINMS, or other) - **Batching:** Do you want HL7 message batching (using [FHS and BHS segments](https://www.lyniate.com/knowledge-hub/hl7-batch-file-protocol/))? - **Timing:** Do you want real time or daily reports, and at what time of day? - **“Ask on Order Entry”:** Do you want “Ask on Order Entry” questions as [OBX fields](https://hl7-definition.caristix.com/v2/HL7v2.5/Segments/OBX)? - **Test data:** Do you require synthetic and/or real patient data before going live? - **Local public health department reporting:** Explain any local public health department reporting requirements that are different from the state. - **Third-party information services:** Do you require your data to be routed through a third-party like a Health Information Exchange or a health information integration service? - **Additional fields:** Any other data or fields you’d like collected? ### Testing facility registration ReportStream collects unique identifiers from testing facilities when they register to send data, including name, [CLIA](https://www.cdc.gov/clia/about.html) number, and address. This information is included with data sent to public health departments. - **Registration requirements:** Do you require testing facilities to register before data is sent to you? - **Custom data fields:** Do you require information in addition to standard fields (name, CLIA number and/or address)? - **Custom registration process:** Do you have a custom testing facility registration process? ### HL7 data fields (Not applicable if using an alternate data format) - Receiving application name - Receiving application [OID](https://www.hl7.org/oid/) - Receiving facility name - Receiving facility [OID](https://www.hl7.org/oid/) ### SFTP details - Staging host name (DNS name) - Staging folder (folder name) - Prod host name (DNS name) - Prod host folder (folder name) ### Document uploads - **Required documentation:** Necessary registration or data use, or legal agreements, etc. that you need ReportStream to complete - **Sample files:** Sample files of your required data formatting, rules or regulations, etc. that would be helpful to review
